Braided Nutella Bread

Prep Time:5 minutes
Cook Time:20 minutes
Total Time:25 minutes
Servings:8
Ingredients:
1 can of crescent roll dough
1/4 cup nutella, or chocolate spread of choice
Powdered sugar (optional)
Procedure:
STEP 1: Preheat oven to 375ÂºF. Unwrap and roll dough onto a clean surface.
STEP 2: Seal perforated edges by gently pinching dough with your fingers.
STEP 3: To make spreading easier, microwave Nutella or chocolate spread for 40 seconds, mixing every 20 seconds.
STEP 4: Cover entire dough with the melted chocolate spread.
STEP 5: Position the dough landscape-wise and roll up the dough tightly. Seal the edges to make sure dough does not unroll.
STEP 6: Move rolled up dough to a baking sheet covered with parchment paper. Leaving one centimeter at one end of the roll, use a sharp knife to cut lengthwise down the middle and completely through the other end.
STEP 7: Gently rotate each half of the dough away from the center so the inside of the roll is facing upwards. Bring one half over the other.
STEP 8: Bring the opposite half over the first half as closely as possible.
STEP 9: Repeat steps 7 and 8 until you run out of dough.
STEP 10: Seal by pinching the two ends together. Bake for 17 to 20 minutes.
STEP 11: Let braided bread cool for 20 minutes.
STEP 12: Finish by dusting powdered sugar on top before serving.
STEP 13: Enjoy :) (as if we had to tell you).
