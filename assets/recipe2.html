Banana Ice Cream

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 30 minutes for “chilling”
Total Time: 45 minutes
Servings: 30 cookies
Feeds: 15 (but one is totally acceptable too because they’re so gosh-darn healthy)
Ingredients:
1/3 cup creamy peanut butter (or try this homemade pb)
2 ripe bananas, mashed*
2 1/2 cups dry oats
Bonus: 1/2 cup chocolate chips
Directions:
1. In a medium bowl, combine peanut butter, bananas, and vanilla extract until well mixed.
2. Mix in oats and chocolate chips.
3. Make into small bowls, put into the fridge, and wait until they are chill.
4. Eat up my friend.
