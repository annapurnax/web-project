Extra easy chocolate truffles

Ingredients:
10 oz chocolate (dark, milk or semi-sweet)
1/2 cup heavy cream
3 tablespoons unsalted butter
Optional:
1 cup chopped nuts
cocoa powder
chopped chocolate
coconut shavings
Directions:
1. Pour the chocolate into a medium sized mixing bowl and set aside.
2. In a heatpoof/microwaveable container, heat the heavy cream and butter for 1 minute and 30 seconds on high or until hot.
3. Pour the heavy cream and butter mixture over the chocolate and let it sit for a minute or two.
4. Mix until the cream is incorporated and chill in the fridge for at least 30 minutes.
5. While the chocolate mixture is chilling, prepare your toppings. Place the chopped nuts, cocoa powder or chopped chocolate into small bowls.
6. Once the chocolate is hard, but still malleable, roll them into little balls. This will get messy and there is no need to roll them into perfect balls. If the chocolate is too soft, refrigerate the mixture for a few minutes before continuing.
7. Roll the chocolate balls into the toppings and set on a baking sheet lined with wax paper. Place the sheet in the fridge to firm up.
